About Al Tair Althahbi

Your trusted key cutting and locksmith partner in Madinat Zayed, Western Region, Abu Dhabi

Who We Are

Al Tair Althahbi Key Cutting L.L.C - S.P.C (الطير الذهبي لنسخ المفاتيح) is a fully licensed and registered key cutting and locksmith company based in Madinat Zayed, the capital of the Al Dhafra (Western) Region of Abu Dhabi.

We specialize in professional key duplication, car key programming, locksmith services, and security solutions for residential, commercial, and automotive clients across the Western Region. Our company fills a critical gap — providing fast, reliable, professional key and lock services to communities that previously had to travel long distances for quality work.

With advanced key cutting and programming equipment, experienced technicians, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we've become the go-to key service provider in Madinat Zayed and surrounding areas.
